Why the Francophone Mobility Program is the Best Option

If you speak French, employers across Canada (excluding Quebec) can hire you without the grueling LMIA process. It's faster, easier, and provides an incredible pathway to permanent residency.

  • LMIA Exempt: Employers are more likely to hire you since they bypass the expensive and time-consuming LMIA process.
  • Pathway to PR: Working in Canada under this program gives you extra CRS points for Express Entry.
  • Bring Your Family: Your spouse may be eligible for an open work permit, and your children can study for free.
